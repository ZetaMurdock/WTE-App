# Paradigm Records

Your **Paradigm** represents your specialized training, combat styles, proficiencies, and access to magical or technology domains.

---

### Scientific Paradigms
Focused on technology, research, and material alteration.
*   **Science:** Uses Hybrid, Medium, and Kinetic weapons. Domains: *Neutral, Elemental*.
*   **Simulation:** Tactical digital hackers using Kinetic and Hybrid weapons. Domains: *Kinetic, Null*.

---

### Esoteric & Survival Paradigms
Focused on remnant artifacts, mutations, or cognitive power.
*   **Remnant:** Artifact hunters utilizing Kinetic, Energy, and Hybrid weapons. Domains: *Kinetic, Neutral*.
*   **Cognition:** Eldritch seekers using Energy, Exotic, and Hybrid weapons. Domains: *Eldritch, Null*.
*   **Evolution:** Xenobiologists mutating their form. Uses Energy, Exotic, and Hybrid weapons. Domains: *Elemental, Eldritch*.

---

### Tactical Combat Paradigms
Direct martial warfare and command.
*   **Warfare:** Frontline operators using Hybrid, Exotic, and Kinetic weapons. Domains: *Neutral, Kinetic*.
