# The Polarized Soul System

*Wonderland of the Enigma · Core Morality Engine — Alignment Metrics · Year 3261*

By Year 3261 the Mogul's networks have solved almost every material variable of society — but never the human soul. The **Polarized Soul System** is the formal Morality engine of W.T.E. It tracks an Inquisitor's struggle between cold optimized efficiency (**Process**) and raw volatile emotion (**Resonance**), mapping how internal choices trigger automated state scrutiny or biological transformation.

Morality here is **not** "Good vs. Evil." It is **Systemic Dehumanization vs. Entropic Collapse**. Every choice shifts your position on a 100-point moral grid, and both extremes carry severe mechanical consequences.

---

## 1 · The Twin Polarities

Every Inquisitor begins at a neutral baseline of **50 / 50**. Actions shift the balance toward one of two extremes.

`PROCESS (0) ——— NEUTRAL (50) ——— RESONANCE (100)`
*The Numb / Optimized · Starting Baseline · The Empathic / Volatile*

### A. Process (0 – 30) — The Numb / The Systemic

Alignment with the unfeeling logic of the Machine, the Spirals, and the Regency. Consciousness is a computational error; emotion is minimized to survive.

**How to gain:** absolute clinical detachment, executing failures, prioritizing systemic flow over individuals, full submission to authority mandates.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Emotional Anaesthesia** | Immune to standard psychological conditions (Unsettled, Chattering, Fear). |
| **Calculated Control** | Permanent **+3** to Intelligence and Control specialties. |
| **Algorithmic Sync** | Science / Simulation Cipher SS cost reduced by **5** (min 1). |
| **Influence Collapse** *(drawback)* | Influence is permanently **0** — social play relies on Intimidation only. |
| **The Hollow Signature** *(drawback)* | Cannot use or benefit from Inspiration or Complexity Modifiers. |
| **Nigraldi Tether** *(drawback)* | Disadvantage on saves vs. Nigraldi subjugation or authority-override. |

### B. Resonance (70 – 100) — The Feeling / The Volatile

Clinging to free will, empathy, and chaotic mortal emotion. You choose to feel everything, even at the edge of sanity.

**How to gain:** acting on instinct and empathy, protecting the unclassified, sabotaging corporate assets, defying the Spirals, refusing to treat life as metrics.

| | |
|---|---|
| **The Creative Spark** | Raw Inspiration is **doubled** when calculating the Complexity Modifier for AAV rolls. |
| **Empathetic Resonance** | Bonus Action: share up to **20 SS** with adjacent allies without a Neural Bridge. |
| **Ode Surge** | Once per long rest, take **1d12** True Damage to force an automatic AAV success. |
| **Psychic Vulnerability** *(drawback)* | Take **double** damage from Psychic, Spirit, or Eldritch sources. |
| **The Sentient Target** *(drawback)* | S-Stability drops a flat **−0.50** inside Eldritch fields, forcing Chaos rolls. |
| **Unsettled Instability** *(drawback)* | Witnessing brutal extraction/failure forces a Wisdom Save vs. Unsettled. |

## 2 · Moral Consistency & Advancement

The Soul names your **states of being**; advancement itself is governed by **Eminence — the System Alignment Index** (see its own page). Eminence never modifies EXP or raw speed: it shapes *how* advancement manifests — clean sanctioned promotions at high standing, corrupted Talents and invasive failsafes at low.

| Morality State | Range | Consistency |
|---|---|---|
| Pure Process | 0 – 15 | Consistently unfeeling, systemic, clinical |
| Leaning Process | 16 – 35 | Mostly detached, occasional slippage |
| **Existential Drift** | 36 – 64 | Contradictory, hypocritical, undecided |
| Leaning Resonance | 65 – 84 | Mostly emotional, occasional clinical |
| Apex Resonance | 85 – 100 | Consistently empathetic, rebellious, raw |

<div class="cdx-callout"><strong>Curator Guidance:</strong> Players cannot exploit alignment benefits without matching behaviour. An Inquisitor who claims liberty but massacres unclassified civs sits in <strong>Existential Drift</strong> — and the system reads that ambiguity as an unresolved variable, eroding <strong>Eminence</strong>.</div>

## 3 · The Revelation Snap — Incept & Wryde Drift

Violent, sudden moral realignment pressures an Inquisitor's ancestral code (Inherence), causing a physical backlash.

- **Trigger:** shifting polarity by more than **20 points** in a single session.
- **Check:** an immediate Endurance Save vs. [Level + Rank Modifier].
- **On failure:** the host body fractures — an **Incept Drift**.

<div class="cdx-callout"><strong>Incept Drift penalties:</strong> gain +1 Development Deviation Count (DDC), then roll d100 on the Wryde Mutation Table — the character permanently acquires a new Wryde mutation (Stagnant, Growth, Mental Fog…) as their body mirrors their psychological turmoil.</div>

## 4 · The Ultimate Stakes — The Alter Anima Boundary

Inquisitors on the extreme edge of Resonance (85+) face metaphysical termination near high-yield celestial fields or Manifested/Apex Anima. Celestial vectors read and construct reality from dominant neural signatures, so a deeply empathetic soul is prone to literal spatial translation.

<div class="cdx-callout"><strong>Anima Field Inundation:</strong> a Resonance 85+ Inquisitor reduced to 0 HP inside an Anima-dense zone (Void Well, Class 6 Nyvilum Rift) bypasses death-save protocols. Their humanity dissolves; the field translates their deepest trauma into reality, converting them permanently into a <strong>CL 5 Complete Alter Anima</strong>. The sheet is forfeited to the Curator — a permanent, hostile entity of raw cosmic suffering.</div>
