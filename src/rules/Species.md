# Species Codex

Inquisitors in WTE are drawn from three primary families: **Humanity**, **Omenity**, and the mysterious **Asternem**.

---

### Humanity
The most populous and adaptable species.
*   **Hyomen:** Highly versatile, balanced. Innate: *Prodigal Mind, Indecisive Body, Indomitable Will, Peak Evolution*.
*   **Voaulton:** Heavy physical and cybernetic integration (+2 PHY, +2 END). Innate: *Chemical Mastery, Robotic Integration, Adaptive Analysis, Energetic Synergy*.
*   **Mirga:** Psychological mimics (+2 INT, +2 AP). Innate: *Perfect Mimicry, Mimetic Adaptation, Illusory Disguise, Emotional Mimicry*.

---

### Omenity
Anomalous lineages touched directly by the Enigma.
*   **Oriyu:** Energy manipulators. Assign stats manually. Innate: *Energy Manipulation, Energy Absorption, Energy Projection, Energy Shielding*.
*   **Insectoid:** Carapaced survivors (+2 END, +2 CHA). Innate: *Regenerative Limbs, Eyeless, Reflexive Assurance, Peak Evolution*.
*   **SubDermin:** Crystalline and terrestrial anomalies (+2 END, +2 DEX). Innate: *Forsaken Touch, Radioactive Anatomy, Terrestrial Knowledge, Living Planet Merge*.

---

### Asternem
Beings manifesting from high-CAS absolute zones.
*   **Inderi:** Elders of deep cognition (+2 WIS, +2 INT). Innate: *Eldritch Physiology, Enhanced Regeneration, Perfect Reflex Calibration, True Eye of Solitude*.
*   **Seraph:** Spatial flyers (+2 DEX, +2 WIS). Innate: *Antimatter Wings, Dance of Displacement, Spatial Rupture, Distant Vision*.
*   **Stygians:** Shadow forms (+2 CHA, +2 WIS). Innate: *Shadow Meld, Umbral Step, Hive Conjugation, Dimensional Flicker*.
