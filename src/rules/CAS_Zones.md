# CAS Zones

**CAS (Cognitive Anomaly Sanction) Zones** dictate the level of physical and mental distortion present in the environment. They determine rules for psychic powers, Enigma hazards, and regulatory limitations.

---

### The Five CAS Levels

1.  **Open:** Standard physical reality. Cognitive anomalies are virtually nonexistent. Normal human technology operates without error.
2.  **Managed:** Slight anomaly leakage. Controlled research zones. Minor fluctuations in mental focus may occur.
3.  **Sanction Zoned:** High cognitive distortion. The environment acts unpredictably. Psi anomalies are highly volatile. Standard physics begins to fail.
4.  **Sealed:** Reality is unstable. Special containment gear is required. Psychic powers trigger random spatial folds or temporal delays.
5.  **Absolute:** Total collapse of local physics. Synaptic fields are torn apart. Void decay is highly accelerated. Human life is impossible without absolute enclosure.
