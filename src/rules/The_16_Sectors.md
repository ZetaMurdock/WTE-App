# The 16 Sectors

*Executive Command Protocol — The Cosmological & Territorial Atlas · Year 3261*
*Official Ledger of the Regency and the Indra Intelligence Network*

The Indra Network tracks every system by cardinal position around the Omniversal Weave and by one of four **Sovereign Status Codes**. When you build an Inquisitor, you choose the Sector in which you joined your Paradigm — it colours your origin, your tech level, and the powers that watch you.

---

### Sovereign Status Codes

| Code | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **On Sequence** | Perfect alignment with the Mogul's logistics. Scripts, quotas, and comms synced to Indra in real time. |
| **Detached** | Temporarily uncoupled from ECP updates. High local autonomy; still pays baseline quotas to avoid intervention. |
| **Rogue** | Completely severed from central command. Hostile to audits, hidden behind temporal interference, outside legal protection. |
| **Excursioned** | Physically folded or hollowed by the Eyeless into synthetic spatial helixes, bypassing normal spacetime geometry. |
