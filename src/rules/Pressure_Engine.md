# Pressure Engine

The **Pressure Engine** is a core environmental and psychological mechanic in WTE. It measures the tension of the current situation and affects character actions, combat mechanics, and hazards.

---

### Pressure States
Pressure consists of four distinct levels, tracked on the [Timeline](wte://rules/Timeline):

1.  **Calm:** Normal conditions. Focus is simple. Rest and recovery proceed at full rate.
2.  **Tense:** Stakes are raised. Characters feel the heat. Gated combat abilities begin to unlock.
3.  **Critical:** A high-danger threshold. Threat zones escalate. Visual indicators pulse red and abilities carry dramatic power bonuses or high costs.
4.  **Catastrophic:** The breaking point. Hazards activate automatically. Synaptic spaces decay rapidly. Survival is the sole priority.

---

### Mechanics & Gated Actions
Certain actions or abilities on your character sheet are **Gated** by Pressure. They can only be activated when the current Pressure is at or above the required state (e.g. "Tense or higher").
*   **Resolution:** Active rolls can resolve pressure buildup, but failure risks triggering catastrophic complications.
*   **Decay & Decay Checks:** Lingering effects and status decay speeds are determined by the Pressure state.
